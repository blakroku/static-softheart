As a leading producer of organic cocoa:

  • We work with firms to produce rules that specify expectations for their produce.
  • We back farm management, transparency, traceability, monitoring, and verification for our cocoa.
  • All the way to crucial sourcing regions, we interact with suppliers and encourage better practices.
  • We assist in describing the process of change among the farmers we work with.

Our Support

We provide assistance to individual farmers so they can relationship and improve the standards are committed to, by producing the best organic cocoa. This assistance includes expertise and technical trainings in the areas of productivity, farm reforms, and environmental impacts.

Our Vision

Leading the sustainable development of Ghana organic cocoa farming indusstry through a holistic approach that is aware of economic sociatal and environmental consideration employing natural and proper organic method of farming for better quality and improved productivity

Mission

To facilitate the development of Ghana organic cocoa farming industry across the value chain using practical, teamwork, focused and well cordinated strategies involving the key stakeholders in an effort to ensure that they are continually satisfied

Goals

Cooperatively produce and market organic cocoa produced exclusively by our noble energetic farmers of the cooperatives
